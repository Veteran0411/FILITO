Limits, Fits and Tolerances

What is Tolerance ?

Permissible variation in the size (dimension) of a component to suit Functional Requirements

How to choose Tolerance?

  • ⬩ Part Function
  • ⬩ Cost of Production
  • ⬩ Productivity (Time)
  • ⬩ Availability of Manufacturing Resources

There are 18 Grades of tolerances

  • ⬩ IT01 to IT04: Gauges, measuring instrument
  • ⬩ IT05 to IT07: Precision Engg applications
  • ⬩ IT08 to IT11: 4General Engineering
  • ⬩ IT12 to IT14: For Sheet metal working
  • ⬩ IT15 to IT16: Casting, General cutting work

Standard Tolerance Unit

⬩ i = 3√D + 0.001D μm
⬩ D (mm) : Geometric mean of the lower and upper limits of a diameter step in which the dimension lies.
